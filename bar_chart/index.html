Tokyo 2020 Olympics

Held in 2021, due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the Tokyo Olympic games was a multi-sport event featuring athletes from around the globe.

Gender equality

Gender equality was a big theme of the Tokyo Olympics, trying to have as close to an equal number of men and women competing as possible. This was the first Olympic games to have men and women competing across all NOCs. However, gender equality was not acheived when it came to coaching, with just 24.8% of coaches being women.
